About me
Background / Experience

Background

I started my career in the world of fine chocolate, running operations for some of the most talented chocolatiers in London. Eight delicious years and a couple of stone later, I decided to move into FMCG and started working for MarsWrigley as part of their Sales team, dealing with both external and internal stakeholders.

After taking part in a Hackathon at MW, I was bitten by the coding bug and decided to start learning code, with a view to building business-specific apps.

Coding

As a humanities graduate of the University of St Andrews, my first love has always been language and literature. Coding is the next step in my exploration of the nature of language and communication.

I started coding with SheCodes, completing their Basic, Plus, Responsive and React courses whilst on maternity leave. This gave me chance to develop skills in the following areas:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JS5
  • React
  • Sass
  • Bootstrap
  • Github
  • APIs
  • Code Editors
  • Flexbox
